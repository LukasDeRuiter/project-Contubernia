Hail Decanus!

You have been tasked by our great emperor Galba to lead your contubernium in holding this newly conquered territory in Germania. It is your responsiblity to feed, train and take care of your men and grow this fortifcation. By what name would you like me to adress you, Decanus?

Your goal is to reach 1000 dignitas(see top-left corner) in as few turns as possible. You earn dignitas through every level your men earn. Levels are earned with four stats: strength, gravitas, cunning and intelligence. Your men can train these each turn, as long as they have enough energy left. You are the Decanus. You earn coin which you can spend upgrading your camp and your men. Two of your men are auxilia. they provide food production so that your contubernium has enough to eat. They can build farms and upgrade crops when needed. Be sure to produce more food than your men use!
Good luck, Decanus!
